The Missing Middle in Canberra

Missing Middle Canberra proposes to change all RZ1 zones to RZ2 and all RZ2 zones to RZ3. This would result in modest increases in density, while legalising vast amounts of housing to be built across the city. Increasing supply of medium density housing is considered the most effective way to increase housing affordability.
